About Our Club

Welcome to Cen-Tex Modelers! Whether you are a veteran pilot or brand new to the hobby, you are welcome at our field. We fly small and large-scale radio-controlled aircraft, including electric, gas, and glow-powered models, helicopters, and park flyers.

Hall Field & Facilities

Our flying site, Hall Field, is leased from the US Army Corps of Engineers and beautifully situated adjacent to Stillhouse Hollow Lake. Maintained proudly by our members, our facilities include:


  • 30' x 300' Geo-Tex all-weather runway & intersecting grass runway
  • The covered John H. Brian Pavilion for shade, picnics, and pit setup
  • Startup stands, setup tables, safety fences, and a spectator viewing area
  • Open daily from 08:00 AM until dusk

Flight Training Program

Want to learn how to fly? Technology has made getting started easier than ever. We offer a structured training environment with club flight instructors to help you take your first steps without losing an aircraft.

Ready to fly? Email us at centexmodelers@gmail.com to be paired up with an instructor.
