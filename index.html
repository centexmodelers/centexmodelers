About Cen-Tex Modelers

Welcome to Central Texas' premier radio-control flying club! We are a group of aviation enthusiasts dedicated to the construction and safe operation of model aircraft. From small electric park flyers and scale gas planes to high-performance sport jets and helicopters, our members fly it all.

Whether you're a seasoned pilot looking for an excellent local field or a beginner eager to learn your first maneuvers, you'll find a welcoming community and plenty of support here.

Our Flying Site Facilities

  • 30' x 300' Geo-Tex all-weather runway and intersecting manicured grass runway
  • The covered John H. Brian Pavilion equipped with setup tables and sun shade
  • Heavy-duty startup stands and pilot safety barriers
  • Spectator viewing areas (Visitors are always welcome to watch!)
  • Open daily from 8:00 AM until dusk
